Get ready to apply

File your income taxes

Receiving the CCB will be required to access this benefit.

Great. You must be the parent who receives CCB payments for your child in order to apply for the dental benefit.

If your spouse or common-law partner receives the CCB for your child, they should complete the next steps.

You must be the parent who receives CCB payments for your child in order to apply for the dental benefit.

Since your spouse or common-law partner receives CCB payments for your child, they should apply for the dental benefit. They should complete the next steps.

You will not be able to apply for this benefit for your child.

Only the other parent or caregiver who receives CCB payments for your child may be eligible. They should complete all the steps.

If your CCB custody arrangement has changed, make sure you update your information with the CRA.

The dental benefit is only available for families receiving the CCB.

If you are not receiving CCB payments, check if you're eligible to apply.
