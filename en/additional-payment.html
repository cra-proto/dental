Additional payment if dental costs are over $650

Status: Opens on July 1, 2023

The interim Canada Dental Benefit provides an additional payment for some eligible children who have higher dental costs. Your additional payment would be the same amount as the period you apply for.

You may meet the criteria if you are not applying for both benefit periods and your child's dental costs are more than $650 in the period you apply for.

Criteria for the additional payment

Check if you can get an additional payment for your child's dental care for the first or second benefit period. For both benefit periods, the earliest you can apply is July 1, 2023.

  • First benefit period: October 1, 2022 to June 30, 2023
  • Second benefit period: July 1, 2023 to June 30, 2024
