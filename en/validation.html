Validating your application

If your application is selected for review, we may ask for more information to validate your application.

On this page

  • Documents you may need to provide
  • How to submit your validation documents
  • If you disagree with the decision

Documents you may need to provide

Keep your receipts for 6 years and make sure your mailing address is up to date. The CRA will contact you by mail if we need more information to validate your eligibility.

You do not need to call us if your child's dental appointment is rescheduled within the same benefit period unless we ask for this information.

If you get a letter from the CRA

You must provide all requested information within 30 days from the date on the letter. If you are found to be ineligible or do not provide the required information, you may have to return any payments you received.

We may request one of the following for each child you applied for:

  • receipt for dental care services
  • invoice for dental care services
  • dental statement of account
  • letter from the dental care provider

Your document must include:

  • name and address of your child
  • name and address of the dental care provider
  • date of the dental appointment
  • cost of the dental care service

The CRA may verify the authenticity of the documents you provide. We may call you or your dental care provider to confirm any information you give us.

How to submit your validation documents

Online
Submit your documents through CRA My Account
  1. Sign in to your CRA My Account
  2. Select "Submit documents" (in left navigation)
  3. Enter your reference number
  4. Select the tax year from the dropdown
  5. Enter a short description and upload your files
By fax
Send your documents by fax
  1. Include a cover sheet with your full name, SIN, case or reference number (if available), a daytime phone number, and the number of pages you're faxing
  2. Label the cover sheet "Supporting documents for Canada Dental Benefit payments"
  3. Fax your documents to 1-833-325-0555
By mail
Send your documents by mail

Canada Dental Benefit
Sudbury Tax Centre
P.O. Box 20000, Station A
Sudbury ON P3A 5C1

How long it takes

Validation can take several weeks from the date we receive all of your additional information.

We will contact you once the validation is complete.

If you disagree with the decision

If your application is denied and you do not agree with the decision, you may ask to have your application reviewed again.

Your request for a second review must be submitted within 90 days of the decision and include the following:

  • The reasons why you disagree with the decision (such as, not all of the information was considered, certain facts or details were missing, or misinterpreted)
  • Any relevant new documents, new facts, or correspondence
  • Your current home address and current phone number

To maintain impartiality and transparency, the second review will not be done by the same CRA official who did the first review.

Request a second review

Online

Sign in to CRA My Account Register

By fax
Send your request by fax
  1. Include a cover sheet with your full name, SIN, case or reference number (if available), a daytime phone number, and the number of pages you're faxing
  2. Label the cover sheet: "Second review for Canada Dental Benefit payments"
  3. Fax your documents to 1-833-325-0555
By mail
Send your request by mail

Canada Dental Benefit
Sudbury Tax Centre
P.O. Box 20000, Station A
Sudbury ON P3A 5C1

If the second review is denied

If you disagree with the result of the second review, you may apply to the Federal Court for a judicial review of the CRA decision within 30 days of the date you received the second review decision.
