Canada Dental Benefit - Closed

Status: Closed

You can no longer apply

The interim Canada Dental Benefit ended on June 30, 2024. You can no longer apply for this benefit, however check if you qualify for the new Canadian Dental Care Plan.

The interim Canada Dental Benefit was intended to help lower dental costs for eligible families earning less than $90,000 per year. Parents and guardians may have been eligible if they paid for dental care for a child under 12 years old who did not have access to a private dental insurance plan.

A tax-free payment of $260, $390, or $650 was available for each eligible child based on what your adjusted family net income was.

The benefit provided up to 2 payments per child for dental care between October 1, 2022 and June 30, 2024.

This interim benefit was administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).
